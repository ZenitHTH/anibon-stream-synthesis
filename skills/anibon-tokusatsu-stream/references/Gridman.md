# Gridman Universe

## Franchise History
*Gridman* is a multimedia franchise originating from Tsuburaya Productions, distinct from the Ultraman series, heavily featuring themes of digital realities, mecha, and kaiju.

- **Original Tokusatsu (1993)**: The franchise began as the live-action series *Denkou Choujin Gridman* (*Gridman the Hyper Agent*). It followed a digital hero battling computer viruses and kaiju within the "Computer World" to protect the real world. (It was later adapted in the West as *Superhuman Samurai Syber-Squad*).
- **Studio Trigger Collaboration (2018–Present)**: After decades of dormancy, Tsuburaya partnered with the acclaimed anime studio **Studio Trigger** (directed by Akira Amemiya) to revitalize the property. This modern era, collectively known as the **Gridman Universe**, shifted the franchise into anime, blending classic tokusatsu pacing and giant robot aesthetics with high-energy 2D animation and deep psychological themes.

## The SSSS. Anime Projects
- **SSSS.GRIDMAN (2018)**: A standalone reimagining focusing on high school student Yuta Hibiki, who must fuse with Gridman to fight kaiju in a mysterious city.
- **SSSS.DYNAZENON (2021)**: The spiritual successor and second series in the shared universe, introducing a new cast controlling the titular mecha Dynazenon, expanding the lore of kaiju eugenicists and emotional trauma.
- **Gridman Universe (2023 Film)**: A major crossover cinematic event that brings the casts of *GRIDMAN* and *DYNAZENON* together as their overlapping realities begin to collapse, serving as the grand culmination of the "SSSS" storyline.
